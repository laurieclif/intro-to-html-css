Walter White

Chemistry Teacher / Meth Cook

Walter White is an over-qualified, downtrodden chemistry teacher. After a terminal lung cancer diagnosis he starts cooking meth to earn money for his family after he dies under the alias Heisenberg, but as time goes on he loses himself and in turn his family.

Skills

  • Chemistry
  • Lying
  • Teaching
  • Intimidation
  • Manipulation
  • Making good meth

Most Unhinged Moments

  2. Telling Jesse he watched Jane die
  3. Setting off a bomb in a nursing home
  4. Putting a hit out on Jesse
  5. Fighting Skylar
